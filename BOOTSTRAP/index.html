Appam at T-Nagar, Chennai

Appam is a popular breakfast dish from south India. Also known with different names in different places like kuzhi paniyaram in Tamilnadu, Paddu in Karnataka, but quite same as Appam or Appe in most of the places. Small, shallow fried balls seasoned with spice and veggies, when served with chutney make a heavenly combo.
